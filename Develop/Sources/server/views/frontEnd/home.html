About Karry Well

After four months working tirelessly on the project, with the aspiration of inspiring youth spirit and professionally self-broadening technological horizons, our Karrywell team is now presenting Karrywell alpha version. It is a shipping system aiming to local small and medium enterprises such as restaurants, franchises or fashion outlets, which are in need of delivery by express but having difficulties in gathering, training and managing their own shipping teams (shippers).

The advantage of Karrywell:

Enable an enterprise to find the nearest shipper withing a certain.

Enable an enterprise to observe the state of shipment during the shipping process.

Provide a visual interface of the shipment via Google map.

Verify receiving by identification codes between enterprises – shippers and shippers – final receivers.

Calculate and recommend the shortest way to save time and money for both enterprises and shippers.

Ensure payment evidently and auto-update payment information.

What we use ?

Karrywell applies various new languages, Nodejs., lonic, AngularJS for instance into particular tasks. Our lodestar is always keeping up with the latest tech-trends for the most effective applications.
